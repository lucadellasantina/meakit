DEGREES_UND        Degree

   deg = degrees_und(CIJ);

   Node degree is the number of links connected to the node.

   Input:      CIJ,    undirected (binary/weighted) connection matrix

   Output:     deg,    node degree

   Note: Weight information is discarded.
